Scenario A

Resepsi Surabaya

Prioritaskan kemudahan keluarga & logistik yang lebih rendah. Cocok karena gereja juga di Surabaya — satu kota, satu perjalanan. Venue premium modern tersedia di kota.

🏛️ Modern Luxury 👨‍👩‍👧 Family Convenient 💰 Budget Lebih Rendah

💡 Kesimpulan Surabaya

Surabaya cocok jika prioritas utama adalah kemudahan keluarga dan biaya logistik lebih rendah. Karena pemberkatan gereja sudah di Surabaya, mengadakan resepsi di kota yang sama mengeliminasi biaya transportasi & akomodasi tamu. Pilihan venue premium (Westin, Sheraton) menawarkan standard luxury yang baik namun dengan nuansa yang lebih korporat dibanding Bali.

Pilih Surabaya jika…
  • Mayoritas tamu dari Surabaya/Jawa Timur
  • Ingin efisiensi logistik (satu kota dengan gereja)
  • Budget ketat — hemat Rp80–120 juta vs Bali
  • Keluarga besar yang perlu aksesibilitas tinggi
Pilih Bali jika…
  • Ingin intimate wedding experience yang tak terlupakan
  • Foto & video hasil luar biasa (cliffside/garden)
  • Konsep Italian coastal garden sangat cocok di Bali
  • Tamu sudah terbiasa destination wedding

How to Use This Dashboard

1

Set Your Wedding Date

Click the date field in the top-right corner of every page. The countdown on the Dashboard will update automatically.

📅
Example
Wedding Date → 17 Oktober 2025
Dashboard shows: "125 days to go"
2

Add Vendors in Master Data

Go to Vendors in the sidebar. Click + Add Vendor. Fill in the details and — most importantly — tick which events this vendor is linked to. They will automatically appear on those event pages.

Example: Adding a Makeup Artist
Name: Candy Sachi Studio
Category: Hair & Makeup
Instagram: @candysachi
Contract URL: drive.google.com/...
Linked Events: ⛪ Church 🌊 Wedding 📸 Pre-Wed
✓ Candy Sachi will auto-appear in Church, Wedding, and Pre-Wedding vendor tables
3

Fill in Quotes on Each Event Page

Open any event page (Church, Engagement, Pre-Wedding, Wedding). Your linked vendors appear automatically. For each vendor, add one or more line items — each with its own price, payment terms, and notes.

5

Schedule Payments for Confirmed Vendors

Once a line item is ✅ Confirmed, a + Schedule Payment button appears below the vendor table. Click it to set installment dates, amounts, and track who paid.

Example Payment Schedule — Prince EO (Rp 20.000.000)
DP 1 · Rp 10.000.000 · Due: 15 Aug 2025 · Paid by: Giorgio
Balance · Rp 10.000.000 · Due: On wedding day · Paid by: —
6

Add Guests & Assign to Events

Go to Guests. Add each guest and tick which events they're attending. The event pages will then show live pax counts pulled from this list.

Use the filter tabs (All / ⛪ Church / 💍 Engagement / etc.) to view guests per event.

7

Track Everything on the Dashboard

The Dashboard KPI cards always reflect live data. The ✅ Confirmed Spend card shows total locked budget across all events.
